Summary

Adekunle Ibrahim Adeyinka was born in Lagos State, Nigeria. He completed his primary school in Delta State before his family relocated in 2008 to Lagos where he completed his secondary education. Then he went further to obtain a degree in Agriculture in the University of Ilorin, Kwara State.

Ibrahim is an organized and confident Nigerian entrepreneur from Lagos, Nigeria. In 2013, he lauched his clothing brand called Donsy clothings together with his farm called AgroXpress where quality and high customer satisfaction are held in high esteem and also contribute significantly to the community growth and development. He his interested in traveling, photography, music, gaming and his hobbies include football, working out, volunteering with NGO's, movies, drawing, having time out with friends, reading, touring new cities.
